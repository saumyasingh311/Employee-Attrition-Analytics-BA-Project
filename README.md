# 🚀 Employee Attrition Analysis & Retention Intelligence System

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee turnover is one of the most expensive challenges organizations face. High attrition impacts productivity, increases hiring costs, and affects organizational stability.

This project was developed to analyze employee attrition patterns, identify key risk factors, and provide data-driven recommendations that help HR teams improve employee retention and workforce planning.

Rather than simply visualizing data, this project follows a complete Business Analyst lifecycle including requirement gathering, process analysis, user story creation, SQL analytics, dashboard development, and business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Human Resource teams often struggle to answer critical questions such as:

* Why are employees leaving?
* Which departments are experiencing the highest turnover?
* Which employee groups are most at risk?
* How do salary, overtime, job satisfaction, and work-life balance influence attrition?
* What actions can be taken to improve retention?

This project addresses these challenges through a structured business analysis and data analytics approach.

# 🔍 Key Insights

### Insight #1

The overall employee attrition rate is approximately 16.12%.

### Insight #2

Research & Development experiences the highest employee turnover.

### Insight #3

Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives show the highest attrition levels.

### Insight #4

Employees aged 26–35 represent the largest attrition group.

### Insight #5

Employees working overtime are significantly more likely to leave.

### Insight #6

Lower compensation levels are associated with higher attrition.

### Insight #7

Job satisfaction and work-life balance strongly influence employee retention.

# 🎯 Business Recommendations

Based on the findings, the following recommendations were proposed:

### Improve Employee Retention Programs

Develop targeted retention initiatives for high-risk employee groups.

### Reduce Excessive Overtime

Monitor workloads and improve work-life balance.

### Strengthen Career Development

Provide learning opportunities, certifications, and promotion pathways.

### Conduct Employee Satisfaction Assessments

Collect employee feedback regularly to identify issues early.

### Implement Predictive Monitoring

Use analytics to identify high-risk employees before resignation occurs.

### Improve Employee Recognition

Introduce reward and recognition programs to increase engagement.
